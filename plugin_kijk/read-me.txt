--- Adding "Kijk" to the dynamic menu ---

1. Place the folder "plugin_kijk" in you praat preferences folder
    a. To find the preferences open a "New Praat script"and type the following:
             writeInfoLine: preferencesDirectory$

2. Restart Praat.

3. When you select your EMA file in Praat's objects window the button "Kijk" will appear last in the dynamic menu.
